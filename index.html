Beginner

Looking to get into running but don't know where to start?

Well our beginner group is the perfect place!

Our beginner groups meet Wednesdays at 6:30PM and Saturday mornings at 11:00AM.

The trails for beginners is 4KM in total and is broken down as follows: 1.5km light jog, 1.5km fast paced walk and the last kilometre is 650M jog and 350M walk to cool down.

All trails are announced in our weekly newsletter so don't forget to sign up!

Intermediate

Intermediate groups meet Monday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings at 5:30PM and the final meeting of the week is Saturday mornings at 9:30AM

Mondays and Wednesdays are both 5KM runs.

Thursdays are a fast paced 3.5KM run with a 1KM cool down walk.

Saturday Mornings consist of a 7.5KM run and a 2.5KM light jog.

Before joining our intermediate group you will need to meet with our fitness consultant to insure this group is right for you.

All trails are announced in our weekly newsletter so don't forget to sign up!

Advanced

Advanced groups meet Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday evenings at 6:00PM.

All sessions are 10KM conisting of 8KM fast paced run and 2KM cool down jog.

Before joining the advanced group it is a requirement that you complete 4 weeks in the intermediate group. It is also a requirement that 2 out of those 4 weeks you hit new personal best times.

All trails are announced in our weekly newsletter so don't forget to sign up!

Coffee Mornings

All members of the Killarney running club are invited to our weekly coffee mornings.

Our coffee mornings are every Sunday morning and are held in J.M Reidys.

So come along, meet new people and refuel for the start of a new week... oh and did we mention your first cup of coffee is on the house!
